2 Fast 2 Furious (2003)

This sequel focuses on ex-police officer Brian O'Conner (Paul Walker),
who relocates from Los Angeles to Miami to start over. Becoming
involved in the street-racing scene in his new city, Brian befriends car-
savvy Tej (Chris "Ludacris" Bridges) and Suki (Devon Aoki), but his
competitive streak ends when federal agents apprehend him and he must
enter into a deal with the FBI. Working with new partner Roman Pearce
(Tyrese), Brian begins a dangerous mission to take down a powerful drug
dealer.
