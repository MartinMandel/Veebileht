The Fast and the Furious

Dominic Toretto (Vin Diesel) enjoys the adrenaline of street car racing
and his fans treat him like a rock star. After a blazing encounter with the
ruthless Johnny Tran, Dom decides to take Brian (Paul Walker), a
newcomer to street racing, under his wing. Dom's sister Mia sees
something she likes in Brian, too. Trouble is, neither of them realize he's
an undercover cop, and Dominic and his rival Johnny Tran are both the
prime suspects in a case involving dirty money and big-rig hijacking.
