Furious 7 (2015)

After defeating international terrorist Owen Shaw, Dominic Toretto (Vin
Diesel), Brian O'Conner (Paul Walker) and the rest of the crew have
separated to return to more normal lives. However, Deckard Shaw (Jason
Statham), Owen's older brother, is thirsty for revenge. A slick government
agent offers to help Dom and company take care of Shaw in exchange for
their help in rescuing a kidnapped computer hacker who has developed a
powerful surveillance program.
