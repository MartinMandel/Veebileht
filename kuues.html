Fast & Furious 6 (2013)

Since Dom (Vin Diesel) and Brian's (Paul Walker) heist in Rio left them
and their crew very rich people, they've scattered across the globe;
however, they must still live as fugitives, unable to return home to their
families. Meanwhile, Agent Hobbs (Dwayne Johnson) has been tracking
a gang of lethally skilled mercenary drivers whose second-in-command is
someone Dom knows. Unable to take them down himself, Hobbs asks
Dom and his crew for help in exchange for full pardons for everyone.
