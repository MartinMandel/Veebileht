Fast & Furious (2009)

Dominic Toretto is forced to come out of hiding when a loved one is
brutally murdered. Heading to LA to track down the culprit, he crosses
paths with Brian O'Connor, the FBI agent whose undercover infiltration of
Toretto's gang led to the motorised miscreant's exile across the border.
The two men temporarily put their differences aside as they investigate
the killing.
