Fast Five (2011)

Ever since ex-cop Brian O'Conner (Paul Walker) and Mia Torretto
(Jordana Brewster) broke her brother Dom (Vin Diesel) out of custody,
they've traveled border to border to evade authorities. In Rio de Janeiro,
they must do one final job before they can gain their freedom for good.
Assembling their elite team of car racers, Brian and Dom know they must
confront the corrupt businessman who wants them dead, before the
federal agent (Dwayne Johnson) on their trail finds them.
